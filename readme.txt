loop or Local Shop is a new gen solution for customers and local shop owners, who can collaborate and do business.

this solution maintains the shop and location details which are used to find the nearest shop for the user and deliver the goods in fast and shortest time with better service capabilities.

the players are shop owners, the customers and the delivery boys which can be put by shops, or loop itself.

the goods are owned by shop and delivered by them we only provide a way to market their items.
